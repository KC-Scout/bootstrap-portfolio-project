Welcome To _the_foundry!

The Foundry is the last of locally owned gyms in the Midwest. Being a staple of fitness and health for over thirty years, our Central US gym(s) have a ton of history and are rich in character. Members always get a great workout in, but at The Foundry, they are part of our community as well. Our personal trainers and staff actually compete in sports such as powerlifting, olympic lifting, strongman, and bodybuilding. The reason we are so successful and people love The Foundry is because of the environment. The equipment and facilities are awesome but what you won’t find anywhere else is the energy in the air. We have a diverse population of members at our gyms but everyone has one thing in common, they’re here to improve themselves and work hard.

Come check out any one of our 10 locations. We are expanding further across the Central US and look forward to becoming a national brand from coast to coast. This year we broke into 3 new states, and are planning expansion into Arkansas, Colorado, Indiana next year. Stay tuned for more updates!
